Frequently Asked Questions

What financing options do you offer?
⮟

We offer various financing options, including loans and lease agreements. Our finance team will help you find the best solution based on your budget.

Do you accept trade-ins?
⮟

Yes! Bring your current vehicle for an evaluation, and we’ll offer you a competitive value to apply toward your new purchase.

Is there a warranty on purchased vehicles?
⮟

Yes, all new vehicles come with a manufacturer’s warranty, and we also offer extended warranty options for used vehicles.

Can I book a test drive online?
⮟

Absolutely! You can schedule a test drive through our website or by contacting our sales team.

Do you offer vehicle servicing and maintenance?
⮟

Yes, we have a full-service department where you can get regular maintenance, repairs, and inspections from certified technicians.

How long does the financing approval process take?
⮟

The approval process usually takes a few hours, but in some cases, it may take up to a day depending on the complexity of the application.

Can I return a vehicle if I change my mind?
⮟

Our return policy varies depending on the vehicle type and purchase terms. Please contact our sales team for specific details.

Do you offer special discounts or promotions?
⮟

Yes! We have seasonal promotions, military discounts, and loyalty rewards. Check our website or visit our dealership for the latest offers.

Can I get pre-approved for financing?
⮟

Yes, we offer pre-approval options to help you understand your budget before visiting the dealership.

What should I bring when purchasing a vehicle?
⮟

You’ll need a valid driver’s license, proof of insurance, and any financial documentation required for financing or trade-ins.
