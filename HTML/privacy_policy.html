Privacy Policy

Last updated: Jan 01, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Yonas Car Dealer. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, process, store, and protect your personal information when you interact with our website and services. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring the confidentiality of your data.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal details when you interact with our services:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Residential or billing address
  • Payment details (secured via third-party providers)
  • Identification information required for financing applications

2.2 Non-Personal Information

We automatically collect certain non-personal data to improve our website’s functionality:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Device type
  • Geolocation data (for localized offers)
  • Website usage statistics, including clickstream data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for various purposes, including:

  • Processing vehicle purchases, financing applications, and service requests.
  • Providing personalized offers based on preferences and location.
  • Improving customer service and website functionality.
  • Communicating important updates, promotions, and dealership news.
  • Enhancing fraud prevention and ensuring security compliance.
  • Analyzing user trends to refine business strategies.

4. Data Protection & Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security protocols to prevent unauthorized access, data breaches, and misuse:

  • Encrypted database storage
  • Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption for transactions
  • Firewall and threat detection systems
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Restricted employee access to personal data
  • Secure authentication measures for sensitive information

5. Data Retention Policy

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

6. Third-Party Sharing & External Links

We **do not sell** personal information. However, we may share data with trusted partners, including:

  • Financial institutions for loan processing
  • Insurance providers for policy assistance
  • Third-party analytics services to improve user experience
  • Legal entities in cases of fraud prevention or compliance enforcement

Our website may contain third-party links, but we are not responsible for their privacy policies or security practices.

7. User Rights & Data Access

You have the following rights under data protection regulations:

  • **Right to Access:** Request details of personal data we store.
  • **Right to Correction:** Update inaccurate or outdated information.
  • **Right to Deletion:** Request the removal of personal data.
  • **Right to Restriction:** Limit how we process certain data.
  • **Right to Portability:** Request a digital copy of data.
  • **Right to Object:** Opt out of marketing communications.

To exercise these rights, contact us at **[Your Contact Email]**.

8. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance user experience and optimize website performance:

  • Session cookies (temporary storage)
  • Persistent cookies (remembering preferences)
  • Analytics cookies (tracking website activity)

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

9. Compliance with Privacy Laws

We adhere to global data protection standards, including:

  • **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)** – Applies to users in the EU
  • **California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)** – Applies to users in California, USA
  • **Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA)** – Applies to users in select regions

If you believe we have violated your privacy rights, you may file a complaint with the relevant data protection authority.
